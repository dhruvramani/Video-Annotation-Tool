How to use this?

  1. Type in the Season and Episode Number, and let the video upload!
  2. Click on Get CSV Data.
  3. The tool will automatically go to timestamps where laughter has been detected in that season.
  4. Click on "Yes" if there really is laughter present in that timestamp. "No" if otherwise.
  5. Click "Next" to go to the next dialogue, and "Prev" for the previous
  6. DON'T FORGET TO PRESS "SAVE" WHEN YOU ARE DONE
